
 # 🧮 Complete Maths Bootcamp

> **Practical maths bootcamp with theory, solved problems, and Python implementations for real understanding.**

A comprehensive guide to mathematical foundations for Machine Learning and Deep Learning, featuring complete theory, detailed derivations, solved problems, and Python implementations.

---

## 📚 What's Inside

### 1. **Calculus for ML/DL**
Master the mathematical foundation of neural networks:
- Derivatives, partial derivatives, and the chain rule
- Gradients and optimization theory
- Backpropagation full derivation
- Loss functions and their gradients
- Activation functions mathematics
- Taylor series and approximations
- Advanced topics: Lagrange multipliers, KKT conditions
- Calculus in Transformers and Attention mechanisms

**Key Topics:** Functions & Limits | Derivatives | Multivariable Calculus | Chain Rule | Optimization | Gradient Descent Variants | Integration & Probability | Matrix Calculus | Backpropagation | Loss Functions

### 2. **Linear Algebra for ML/DL**
The language of data, tensors, and neural networks:
- Vectors, matrices, and tensors from scratch
- Matrix operations and special matrices
- Systems of linear equations
- Vector spaces and subspaces
- Norms, projections, and distances
- Eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and diagonalization
- SVD, PCA, and matrix decompositions
- Positive definite matrices
- Applications in neural networks and transformers

**Key Topics:** Scalars & Vectors | Matrix Operations | Determinants | Eigendecomposition | SVD | PCA | Linear Transformations | Dimensionality Reduction | Neural Network Applications
